In this release we are introducing a brand new reservation widget that you can place on your website. Not only does it come with a new style and different orientation options, it collects more information about your guests, such as email, food preferences, allergy, event type and more. Now every restaurant in our system has a page with information about the restaurant and an online reservation form. Restaurants can add a link to this page to their website or to the restaurant's profile on Yelp, Google, TripAdvisor or any other business catalogs, to make it easy for visitors to book a table on-the-go. Read about all these improvements below.
Restaurant Reservation Page
The page offers visitors information about the restaurant, such as a short profile, phone number, website, opening hours, address and location on a map. An online reservation form helps guests to quickly book a table.
Collect More Information about Your Guests
Now we help you collect not only a guest's name and phone number, but his or her email, food preferences, allergy, event type and special requests such as a stroller or high chair.
Example of the Widget on a Restaurant's Website
With just a few lines of JavaScript code, you can place a reservation widget on your website (Wix, Wordpress) or Facebook pages. You can find the code in the reservations section on the administrative portal.
