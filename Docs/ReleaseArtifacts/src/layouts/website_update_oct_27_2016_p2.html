New Reservation Widget

Now every restaurant in our system has a page with information about the restaurant and an online reservation form. Restaurants can add a link to this page to their website or to the restaurant's profile on Yelp, Google, TripAdvisor or any other business catalogs, to make it easy for visitors to book a table on-the-go.

NEW: Restaurant Reservation Page

The page offers visitors information about the restaurant, such as a short profile, phone number, website, opening hours, address and location on a map. An online reservation form helps guests to quickly book a table

NEW: Collect More Information about Your Guests

Now we help you collect not only a guest's name and phone number, but his or her email, food preferences, allergy, event type and special requests such as a stroller or high chair.
